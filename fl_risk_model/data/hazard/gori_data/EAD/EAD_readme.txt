Synthetic Expected Annual Damage

The expected annual damage is calculated as the average yearly damage based on the synthetic hazards (wind, rain, surge), exposure (county GDP) and the regression models (coefficients presented in supplementary information). 

Files with "_pop_wealth" utilize ICLUS county-level population projections to 2020 and assume a 2% GDP per capita growth rate (from 2020 dollars) to 2080. These files represent the scenario of future socioeconomic growth.

AAL  = Expected Annual Damage (EAD) for each county. Counties are listed in the "US_counties.shp" file 
AAL_tot = EAD for the entire US
